PawPatrol

Paw Patrol is a Canadian animated television series created by Keith Chapman and produced by Spin Master Entertainment, with animation provided by Guru Studio. In Canada, the series is primarily broadcast on TVOntario as part of the TVOKids programing block.

Peppa Pig

Peppa Pig is a British preschool animated television seriesthat follows Peppa, an anthropomorphic female piglet, and her family, as well as her peers portrayed as other animals. Peppa is a loveable, cheeky little piggy who lives with her little brother George, Mummy Pig and Daddy Pig.

Bluey

Bluey is an inexhaustible six-year-old Blue Heeler puppy. She lives with her dad, mum, and four-year-old little sister, Bingo. Bluey loves to play and turns everyday family life into extraordinary adventures. The show is built around imaginative games led by Bluey and Bingo.
