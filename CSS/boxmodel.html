Roblox

Roblox is an online game platform and game creation system developed by Roblox Corporation, allowing users to program and play games created by themselves or other users. Launched in 2006, it has become a popular platform, especially among children and teenagers, with over 100 million monthly active users.

Rolls Royce

Rolls-Royce is a British luxury automobile maker that has operated as a wholly owned subsidiary of BMW AG since 2003. They develop and deliver complex power and propulsion solutions for safety-critical applications in the air, at sea, and on land.
